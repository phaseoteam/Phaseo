Payment failures are usually caused by bank authorization blocks or stale card details.

## What to check

- Card number, expiry date, and CVC are current.
- Billing address matches your card statement.
- Your card supports online and recurring transactions.

## Next steps

1. Retry the payment after updating details.
2. Use a different payment method if retries fail.
3. Contact your bank and ask for merchant authorization release.
