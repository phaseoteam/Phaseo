description: How plan changes are applied and when billing updates take effect.

---

You can change plans from **Settings > Billing**.

## Plan change behavior

- Upgrades usually apply immediately.
- Downgrades often apply at the next billing cycle.
- Proration depends on your current cycle and plan rules.

## Before changing plans

- Confirm feature differences and limits.
- Validate impact on active team members and API usage.
