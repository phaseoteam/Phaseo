To review billing documents:

1. Open **Settings > Billing**.
2. Locate the invoice history section.
3. Download invoices or receipts for the required period.

## Missing invoice?

- Confirm you are in the correct workspace/team.
- Check whether the charge is still pending.
- Contact support with transaction date and amount.
