Use this checklist to harden your account:

- Enable MFA on every admin or billing account.
- Use a unique password managed by a password manager.
- Review recent sign-in events regularly.
- Remove stale sessions and unused devices.
- Limit access by role and least privilege.

## Team guidance

- Remove access immediately when team members leave.
- Avoid shared accounts.
- Require MFA for all privileged roles.
