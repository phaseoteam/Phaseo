You can control analytics cookies from the cookie banner and settings prompt.

## Consent options

- **Accept** enables analytics integrations (for example GA and PostHog).
- **Deny** disables non-essential analytics cookies.

## Update your choice later

- Reopen cookie settings from the site footer or consent prompt.
- Save your new preference.
- Reload the page if analytics state does not update immediately.

## Notes

- Required cookies used for authentication or core functionality remain enabled.
- Consent preferences are stored per browser profile.
