---
title: Suspicious Sign-In Activity
description: Steps to take if you suspect unauthorized account access.
order: 3
updated: 2026-02-06
---

If something looks suspicious, secure the account immediately.

## Immediate actions

1. Change your password.
2. Sign out other active sessions.
3. Re-enroll MFA if you suspect compromise.
4. Review account settings for unexpected changes.

## Contact support when needed

Include:

- account email
- timestamp of suspicious activity
- affected resources or settings
- screenshots of relevant errors or events
