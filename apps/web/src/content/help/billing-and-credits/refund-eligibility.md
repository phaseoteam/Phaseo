---
title: Refund Eligibility
description: When a credit purchase is eligible for self-serve refunds.
order: 1
updated: 2026-02-09
---

Credit purchases are eligible for self-serve refund within 24 hours if none of the purchased credits were consumed.

A purchase becomes ineligible when:

- The 24-hour window has passed.
- Any credits from that purchase have been used.

You can review status from `Settings -> Credits`.
