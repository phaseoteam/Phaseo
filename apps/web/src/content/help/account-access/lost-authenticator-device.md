title: Lost Your MFA Device

If you lose access to your authenticator app, you will not be able to complete MFA verification.

## Immediate steps

1. Try to restore your authenticator app from backup or cloud sync.
2. Check whether your old device is still available and can produce valid codes.
3. If you can sign in on another trusted session, rotate MFA immediately.

## Recovery when you are locked out

- Open a support request and select the account access option.
- Include:
  - account email
  - date of last successful sign-in
  - a brief explanation of what changed (lost device, reset phone, etc.)

Support may require ownership verification before MFA can be reset.

## After account recovery

- Re-enable MFA right away.
- Add at least one backup device where possible.
- Store emergency access instructions in your internal runbook.
